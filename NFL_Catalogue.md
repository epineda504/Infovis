Column	Description
schedule_date	Game date
schedule_week	Game week in season
schedule_playoff	Is a playoff game? (TRUE/FALSE)
team_home	Home team
score_home	Home team points scored
team_away	Away team
score_away	Away team points scored
stadium	Stadium name
stadium_neutral	Neutral venue? (TRUE/FALSE)
weather_temperature	Temperature (°F)
weather_wind_mph	Wind speed (mph)
weather_humidity	Humidity (%). Indoor games have no data.
weather_detail	indoor, rain, snow, fog, etc.
